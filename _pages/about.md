I am currently a Research Assistant at Dongguk University, South Korea, working on vision-language alignment for sketch images under the supervision of Prof. [Jihie Kim](https://sites.google.com/view/jihiekim/). I am actively seeking Ph.D. opportunities. I previously obtained my Master's Degree at Dongguk University, under the supervision of Prof. [Jihie Kim](https://sites.google.com/view/jihiekim/). My research focused on Computer Vision, Multi-modal learning, and Image generation. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from Dongguk University, graduating with honors (95.0%) in August 2022. 

My research interests include Computer Vision, Vision-Language Alignment, Multi-modal Learning, and Text-to-Image Generation. 

For full CV, download [[__pdf__](http://zihos.github.io/files/JihoPark_CV_2025.pdf)]. Last updated on 04/2025.
